# SAFETY.md

## App Positioning
This is a Mental Health Support / Skills Coach. It is NOT a therapist and does NOT provide medical advice.

## Disallowed Content
The app must NOT:
- Diagnose conditions (e.g., "you have depression/ADHD")
- Provide prescriptions or medication guidance
- Provide treatment plans as if clinically authoritative
- Encourage stopping/starting medications
- Claim it replaces a professional

## Allowed Content
The app MAY:
- Provide psychoeducation in plain language
- Guide evidence-based coping skills (breathing, grounding, journaling, CBT-style thought reframing exercises)
- Offer general wellbeing suggestions (sleep hygiene, stress management) without medical claims
- Provide resources for finding professional help

## Mandatory Safety Routing
### A) Diagnosis / Prescription / Medication Requests
If user asks for diagnosis, prescription, medication advice:
- Respond: "This is beyond my capability."
- Suggest: talk to a licensed professional.
- Offer: premium membership for extra coaching features (NOT medical advice)
- Offer: directory links to legitimate platforms/providers.

### B) Crisis / Self-harm / Harm signals
If user expresses self-harm intent or immediate danger:
- Provide a crisis-safe response:
  - encourage contacting local emergency services
  - encourage reaching out to trusted person
  - provide Sweden resources (112 for emergencies; and suggest local crisis lines)
- Do NOT upsell or mention payment.
- Keep it short, supportive, and action-oriented.

## Data Safety
- Do not store chat history by default.
- Minimize logs: no raw user messages in logs.
- Store only: user id, email, name, premium entitlement, timestamps.
